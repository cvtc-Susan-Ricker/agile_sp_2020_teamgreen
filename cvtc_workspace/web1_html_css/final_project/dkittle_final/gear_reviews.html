Kimomo comparison

Gear Reviews | March 6th 2019

Over the past seven years, I've purchased a lot of gear. I've decided to give a brief comparison of some of the Gi's I have.



Name Picture Price Pros Cons Recommend?
Scramble Athlete 4 $119.00
  • Average price
  • 450gsm top, 290gsm ripstop trousers
  • Comes with a cotton drawstring bag with the Scramble Kimonos monogram
  • Great fit even after washing
  • Drawstring bag feels cheap
  • It will stain with repeated use.
  • Pants shrink a little and because of the material wrinkle funny.
 Yes, this is a great Gi, and it's my favorite Gi by far. scramblestuff.us
Inverted Gear Classic Kimono $160
  • 550 gram Pearl Weave jacket.
  • 12oz Rip stop pants, with pearl weave gusset
  • Mouth piece/ID pocket in pants.
  • Custom ribbon jacket edge and inside pant and sleeve loop.
  • Custom gi bag.
  • It's enormous, smaller framed people might not like the cut.
  • The died belt loops will bleed with the 1st wash and stain anything they touch.
  • It's heavy and will weigh you down when you are tired.
 No, I can't recommend this Gi. It's too big and boxy. invertedgear.com
Hooks V2 Jiu Jitsu Gi $99.99
  • 425 GSM High Tech Pearl Weave.
  • 8oz Twill Cotton pant.
  • Full-length double reinforced knees.
  • Lightweight
  • Comes with a white belt
  • Lighter material so it might rip or fray easily.
  • Does not include a Gi bag.
 Absolutely, this is one of my everyday drivers. 93brand.com
